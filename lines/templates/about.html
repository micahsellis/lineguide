About the Line Guide

In a world used to "Instant Gratification", people HATE lines!
Line Guide was created to help you navigate through the lines at nearby establishments. You no longer have to be mislead by establishments with incorrect wait times or waste precious time of your life waiting in lines!!
We hope that with this app, you get a chance to explore new places, and help your fellow humans out, by adding wait times and creating lines!


Frequently Asked Questionsmore_vert
FAQsclose

1. I can't find the line I'm looking for.

Line Guide gives you the ability to create a new line! You must be logged in to add a new line. Simply click login/signup link at the top of the screen and login or create a new account! Once you have logged in you can click on Create NEW Line and add information about the business you want to create a line for.

2. How can I see all the wait times for a line?

Navigate to a business details page and click the 3 dots in the top right of the average wait time card. Here you can add a new time or view all the wait times submitted by other users.

3. Can I edit a line or change a picture?

Lines can only be edited by you, if you created the line. Navigate to the business details page and at the bottom, click on the Update Line link to make changes to your line.
